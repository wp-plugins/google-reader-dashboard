== Description ==

*NOTICE*
Google Reader Dashboard is currently broken as of version 1.1.1 and is being rewritten to adapt to some changes in Google's API. Please check this space for version 1.2 when it becomes available.

Add your Google Reader feed to the WordPress admin dashboard.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `google-reader-dashboard` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Login to the widget with your Google username (gmail address) and password.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Are you stealing my Gmail password? =

No.  For future use, your account information is stored in a cookie on your local machine.  This way you don't have to log in again for a while, but we never send this information anywhere else.  For your protection, the cookie will automatically expire after 7 days.
